About Us

Our story, our people, our values

Our Story

PM LUXURY COURT was founded in 2025 by the Aurelia family with a simple belief — that every guest deserves to feel at home, only much better.

What started as a bold vision is growing into a luxury destination, recognised for its hospitality, cuisine, and comfort.

Today, we continue to uphold the founding values of warmth, quality, and genuine care.

Why Choose Us

Here is what sets PM LUXURY COURT apart from the rest:

  1. Established in 2025 with a commitment to exceptional hospitality
  2. Award-winning on-site dining and spa facilities
  3. Personalised service tailored to every guest
  4. Central Bibiani location close to business and culture
  5. Consistent 5-star guest reviews year after year

Our Values

  • Hospitality: We treat every guest like family.
  • Quality: We never compromise on comfort or service.
  • Community: We support local suppliers and Ghanaian culture.
  • Sustainability: We operate with care for the environment.

Our Team

Every member of our team is trained to deliver service that exceeds expectations.
